Courses We Offer

We have different programs in your choice course of study.

Diploma

We have a wide variety of courses in our ND and HND programs. Join us as we help you to get a Diploma degree in your choice course of study. Note that ND and HND runs for 2 years each.

Part-time Undergraduate

We also have a part-time program suitable for you if you are a working student, or you are faced with distance learning,etc. Join us to get your part-time honours in either as Diploma, or as Degree in your desired course.

Full-time Undergraduate

We have a huge selection of courses for you in our Full-time Undergraduate programs, from the Medicals, Engineering, Pure Sciences, Socials to even the Arts. Join us as we help acheive your dream in getting that degree in your desired course.

Post-Graduate

Our Post-Graduate program will help you get further honours in your choice courses as we also have a wide variety of courses in it too. Join us to help you acheive further honours in that your choice course of study
