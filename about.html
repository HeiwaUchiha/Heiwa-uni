About Us

We are one of the best universities in Nigeria

Heiwa University was founded in 2003 by a group of high profile members of the Nigerian Society, with the aim of making Tetiary Education affordable, suitable and comfortable for the common Nigerian university aspirants. It was named after the pioneer of the idea, Dr. Heiwa Uchiha who a leading scientist in the world, doing his best for his country. Ever since then, the University has become popular nationwide and internationally, attracting various sponsorships from international organizations, giving it the opportunity to be deliver quality education to students who are graced to apply. Heiwa University has also produced many men and women who have gone on to make the school, nation proud; domestically and in the diaspora.
